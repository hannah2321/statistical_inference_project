State assumptions and conclusions.

Assumptions: equal variance, not paired samples Null Hypothesis: no difference in impact of OJ v. VC

Conclusions: In comparing the OJ supplement versus the VC supplement, there do not appear to be any significant differences in the impact on len. The p-value of 0.06063 is larger th an alpha (0.05) and the null hypothesis is not rejected. In comparing the 3 levels of doses, there does appear a significant impact of the level of dose. Each paired t-test revealed a p-value less than 0.05 leading to the null hyopothesis being rejected.
